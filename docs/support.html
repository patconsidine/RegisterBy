Common questions

Where is my data? On your iPhone only. We do not run servers or accounts for RegisterBy.

Restore Pro purchase Open RegisterBy → Settings → upgrade screen → Restore purchases (same Apple ID as the original purchase).

Reminders not firing? Check Settings → Notifications for RegisterBy, and your reminder toggles inside the app.

Registration links Manufacturer URLs are shortcuts only; always confirm deadlines on your receipt or the brand’s site.
