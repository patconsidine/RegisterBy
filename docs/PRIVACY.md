# RegisterBy Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 2026

RegisterBy ("the app") is operated as a personal warranty reminder tool.

## Data we collect

We do **not** collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. There is no account, no analytics SDK, and no advertising network.

## Data stored on your device

- Product names, dates, notes, and warranty details you enter
- Photos of receipts and serial plates you choose to attach
- Your Pro purchase status (via Apple)

This data stays on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData and the app sandbox. It is not uploaded to our servers because we do not operate servers for this app.

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, reminders are scheduled locally on your device for your items only.

## Third parties

- **Apple App Store** processes in-app purchases under Apple's privacy policy
- **Manufacturer websites** open in Safari when you tap registration links; their policies apply
